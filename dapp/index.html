FAQ

What are the Squirrel Degens?

Squirrel Degens are unique NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain. They are created as ERC-721S tokens and bring many investor benefits.

When can I buy Squirrel Degens?

Public Sale and Whitelist Sale date TBA at a later date. We will launch as soon as the time is right.

How much does the NFT cost?

Whitelist Sale: 0,04 ETH.
Public Sale: 0,05 ETH.

Which wallets are supported on mint?

You can mint using a Coinbase-, Rainbow-, Trust-, Argent-, Metamask-, Safe-, imToken wallet, or any wallet supported by Wallet Connect.

Where can I buy Squirrel Degens?

Minting will be only on our website www.squirreldegens.com

What are the investor benefits?

The Squirrel Degens NFT will give you access to internal company documents and processes that no one else would give you. From standardized processes, sales documents, and advertising strategies to marketing psychology, everything is included.

Become a part of a whole eco system for enterpreneurs, to let them kickstart their business!

How many can I buy?

Our goal is to build a large and long-lasting community behind the Squirrel Degens. Therefore we have decided to allow all whitelist buyers to mint only 2 NFTs per wallet and transaction and all public sale participants 5 NFTs per wallet and transaction.

How many NFTs will be available?

1183 NFTs will be available for the public sale, 2000 for the whitelist, sale and 150 for the team and special supporters of the project.

How can I legally use my own Squirrel Degen NFT?

You can use your NFT wherever you want which means that we dedicate all Squirrel Degens NFTs to the public by waiving all our rights in the work worldwide under copyright law, including all related and neighboring rights, to the fullest extent permitted by law.

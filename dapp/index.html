About the project

Become a founding member of an ecosystem that will disrupt the hotel industry through NFT technology.

We will build latespot.com, the booking app that will transform the hotel market of the future.

Supported by the Swiss government and one of the world's leading universities for technology research (FHNW), latespot aims to shape the future of late-night check-ins at hotels after midnight as a hotel booking app.

There is no way to book a hotel room after midnight for the same night online. Latespot will allow you to book a room after midnight for the same night at a reduced price. And hotels can fill any remaining room and profit as well.

The application uses blockchain technology and NFTs as upgradable and tradable digital user identities that are fully owned by the end user of the application. The NFT and blockchain technology helps us redesign selected features of the application in a fairer and more user-centric way.

Each Squirrel Degens NFT acts as a non-fungible and tradable user identity, giving holders or our so-called founding members access to many real-life benefits. mint details

The ERC-721S contract
‍
We have programmed our own contract for the mint, which is based on the ERC-721 standards.
We call it the ERC-721S contract (S for Squirrels).
‍
The mint cost of one NFT will hardly or not at all differ from a mint of multiple NFTs.
‍
You only have to pay the costs to write the wallet address to a database, which costs much less, because this process takes less code and therefore less gas fees.
‍
After all NFTs are reserved on our database, we will mass-mint all NFTs within 96 hours.
‍
Overall, our contract and approach will result in much lower gas costs.

FAQ

What are the Squirrel Degens?

Squirrel Degens are unique NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain. They are created as ERC-721S tokens and bring many investor benefits.

What is ERC-721S and why is it so good?

ERC-721S (S stands for Squirrels) is our own contract that we created to significantly reduce gas fees. You only pay gas fees to write your address on a data structure (HashMap).

We will take over all the minting gas fees that the user normally pays through our contract, as we will mint all 10,000 NFTs in bulk in a 96 hour period after the sellout. We plan a maximum of 96 hours because we need to optimize our gas fees that we pay for you as well. We think this should be the new industry standard.
